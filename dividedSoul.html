Divided Soul

Project type:

12h Game jam

Team:

3 people

Game engine & Languages:

Unity, C#

Tools:

Git, Aesprite

Divided Soul comes from a 12 hours game jam. The theme was "Stronger Together". This is a 2D puzzle game in cooperation. You play two split souls which have to merge together to become complete (and complete the level!). Players play on different sides of the same keyboard (like an old-school multiplayer experience!) We managed to come up with the concept of the game and create 2 complete levels in 12h. In my case, I also discovered Unity and C# for the first time and had to learn on the spot.

Pressure plate system

In the second level, pressure plates can detect wether a player is entering or exiting their body. If the player's color correspond to the pressure plate's color, then it opens or closes its linked door.

Players merge to complete a level

The blue player detect wehter the red player is entering its body, and do the "merging" by :

  • Set the blue player's position to the red's position
  • Hiding the red player
  • Stop any movements
  • Replace the current blue animation by the fusion animation
