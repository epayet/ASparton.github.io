Towa

Tools :

Towa is a plate game invented by my formation which oppose two players. Their goal is, at the end of the pre-define number of rounds, to have more pieces on the board than their opponent. The project is divided in two parts :

  • Players actions developement in solo (coded in french for formation's purpose)
  • Developement of an AI in duo (coded in french for formation's purpose)

Player's actions and consequences implemented and tested

Diferent actions were possible including :

  • Put a piece on the board
  • Put a piece on another ally piece
  • Activate a tower (remove all lower towers that are on the same row, column or adjacent to it)
  • Creating signals between two towers (remove all lower towers that has a perpendicular signal)...
